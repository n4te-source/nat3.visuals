// about.md

nat3_Visuals — the person behind the work

I'm a designer-developer based in Nigeria — one of those people who got tired of the gap between how things are designed and how they actually get built.

So I learned both. I design in Figma, prototype with Figma Make, and build with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. When you work with me, you get one person who owns the entire process — from the first wireframe to the deployed product.

I work with small businesses, startups, and founders who need someone they can trust to get it done right without having to manage a team of people or explain things twice.

Professional, reliable, and genuinely easy to work with — that's the standard I hold myself to on every project.

discover()

Before I touch Figma, I ask the right questions. What's the actual problem? Who's the user? What does success look like? Good design starts with understanding, not aesthetics.

// 02

design()

Wireframes first, then high-fidelity. I loop you in at every stage so nothing is a surprise at the end. You'll see the thinking behind every decision, not just the output.

// 03

build()

I write and ship clean, fast, production-ready code. No handoff to another dev, no translation loss — what we designed is what gets built. Deployed, tested, and yours.
